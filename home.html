About Me

My name is Pedro. My main interests are comics, math, video games, space, and Linux. The design of this website gives a very nice picture of what type of person I am, and I'm happy that you took time out of your day to take a look at it

My main focus is on close-to-the-metal languages like C, C++, and Assembly, but I like to feel fancy with Lua and Python from time to time. My front-end skills are nothing to scoff at either, with the stars being rendered right on your screen now, made entirely with pure CSS, same for the typing animations, and the whole logic of the site built in pure JavaScript.

I've been using Linux as my daily driver for some time now, getting a really good grasp at the powers of the terminal. My entire workflow is centered around my keyboard and Neovim configuration, with Bash scripts tying everything together.
